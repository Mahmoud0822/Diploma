About Olympia Sport

Established in 1990, Olympia Sport is a family-owned business dedicated to providing high-quality sports equipment and playgrounds throughout Egypt and the Middle East.

We manufacture sports equipment and playgrounds, supply and apply sports flooring including rubber tiles and acrylic surfaces, and serve as the sole right agent for Holzhof playgrounds and Banfer gymnastics in Egypt.

Our vision is to be the leading company in the Middle East, cooperating with major companies in our field to retain the latest technology and highest market share, while offering integrated complete solutions with the highest level of technical support.
